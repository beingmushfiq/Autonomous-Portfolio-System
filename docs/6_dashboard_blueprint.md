# Autonomous Portfolio System: Owner Dashboard UI Blueprint

The private dashboard is the control center for the Autonomous Portfolio System. Unlike the public-facing portfolio, this is an administrative hub focused on insights, analytics, approvals, and opportunity tracking.

---

## Overall Layout System

- **Navigation**: Sidebar styling (collapsible on mobile) containing: Home, Inbox (Review Queue), Opportunities, Graph Visualizer, Settings.
- **Theme**: Sleek Dark Mode (Tailwind `.dark`). Uses glassmorphic panels and subtle gradients to feel high-end.
- **Dashboard Layout**: CSS Grid approach. A primary column for actionable items, and a secondary column for real-time telemetry. 

---

## 1. Top Bar: Quick Stats & Current Status

Always visible at the top.
- **System Status**: "🟢 Automation Engine: Active", "🟢 GitHub webhook: Connected".
- **Reputation Score**: A calculated integer (e.g., `874`) with an up/down weekly trend indicator (+12%).
- **Active Visitors**: "3 visitors currently viewing portfolio".

---

## 2. Primary Column (Action & Intelligence)

The center space for items requiring the owner's attention.

### A. The "Review Queue" (Inbox)
The system works on its own, drafting content. The owner simply approves, edits, or rejects.
- **Newly Drafted Case Studies**: "Project: `crypto-bot` ingested. Case study ready for review. [Approve & Publish] [Edit Draft]"
- **Article Suggestions**: "Writing Engine suggests: '3 Lessons Learned scaling Node.js'. [Generate Full Article]"
- **Content Distribution**: "LinkedIn post drafted based on your recent article. [Post Now]"

### B. AI Insights Generator
A dynamically populated summary generated by analyzing the portfolio's recent growth.
- **Card Title**: "Weekly Growth Analysis"
- **Content**: 
  - *"Your proficiency in Next.js has grown 15% this month based on recent commits."*
  - *"Recruiters are spending 40% more time on the 'Machine Learning' tag than last week."*
  - *"Suggestion: You haven't updated your 'Go' projects in 6 months. Consider a new build to maintain the graph node."*

---

## 3. Secondary Column (Opportunities & Telemetry)

Real-time data feeds and tracking.

### A. Opportunity Engine Alerts
When the Intent Analysis flags a high-value visitor session.
- **Red Alert Card**: "🔥 High-Intent Visitor Detected"
  - Intent: Recruiter
  - Activity: Viewed 'Resume' (3x), Spent 5 mins on 'React Native' case studies, downloaded PDF.
  - Location: San Francisco, CA.
  - Recommended Action: *Ensure calendar booking form is active.*

### B. Visitor Funnel Analytics
A simplified, visually appealing conversion funnel.
- Total Unique Hits (Last 7d)
- Engaged Visitors (>2 mins on site)
- Intent Classified (Recruiter: 40%, Dev: 50%, Client: 10%)
- Conversions (Emails sent, Calendars booked, LinkedIn clicks)

---

## 4. UI Components & Micro-Interactions

- **Cards**: Use Tailwind `backdrop-blur-md bg-white/5 border border-white/10` to create frosted glass layers over a deep black background.
- **Buttons**: Hover states use Framer Motion scale effects (`whileHover={{ scale: 1.05 }}`) with a soft glowing `box-shadow` to simulate neon.
- **Empty States**: If the review queue is empty, show a soothing illustration of a robot resting, with text: "The system is scanning for new input. You can relax."
